Eliobot is equipped with a buzzer, allowing it to play musical notes.

## Play a note

![Play note](/img/elioblocs/blocs/sounds/jouer-note.png)

**Type**: Order

**Definition** :

This block allows you to play a musical note. The note is defined by its name (Do, Re, Mi, Fa, Sol, La, Si), its octave, and its duration.

**Use** :

If you want to make your own music.

## Play a frequency

![Play frequency](/img/elioblocs/blocs/sounds/jouer-frequence.png)

**Type**: Order

**Definition** :

This block allows you to play a frequency. The frequency is defined in Hertz (Hz) and the time it is played for.

**Use** :

If you want to play a specific frequency.

## Silence

![Play silence](/img/elioblocs/blocs/sounds/silence.png)

**Type**: Order

**Definition** :

This block allows you to take a break from the music. The pause time is defined in milliseconds.

**Use** :

If you want to take a break from the music you compose.

## Change volume

![Change volume](/img/elioblocs/blocs/sounds/modifier-volume.png)

**Type**: Order

**Definition** :

This block allows you to modify the volume of the buzzer. Volume is defined as a percentage.

**Use** :

Lower the volume so as not to annoy others.

## Play preset music

![Play predefined musics](/img/elioblocs/blocs/sounds/jouer-musique.png)

**Type**: Order

**Definition** :

This block allows you to play predefined music. There are several predefined music available.
- **Imperial March**: The music of Darth Vader in Star Wars.
- **Totally Spies**: The call of Sam, Clover and Alex.
- **Police**: The police siren.
- **Pirate of the Caribbean**: The music of Jack Sparrow.
- **Pink Panther**: The music of the Pink Panther.
- **The Simpsons**: Music from The Simpsons.

**Use** :

If you want to play music without composing it.
